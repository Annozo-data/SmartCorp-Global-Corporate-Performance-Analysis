# SmartCorp-Global-Corporate-Performance-Analysis
## TechCrush Data Analytics Capstone Project

### Project Overview

This project analyzes the Forbes Global 2000 Companies 2025 dataset to help SmartCorp identify growth opportunities, optimize investments, benchmark corporate performance, and support strategic decision-making across industries and regions.

The analysis focuses on company sales, profits, assets, market value, industry performance, geographic trends, and investment opportunities.

---

## Business Problem

SmartCorp needed insights into:

* Top-performing companies globally
* Most profitable industries
* Regional corporate performance
* Relationships between assets, profits, sales, and market value
* Potential investment and partnership opportunities

---

## Data Cleaning

Data cleaning was performed using Microsoft Excel and Power Query.

### Key Cleaning Tasks

* Checked for duplicates
* Validated missing values
* Standardized column names
* Corrected corrupted records
* Removed invalid sales entries
* Formatted data types
* Created analytical columns for deeper insights

### Calculated Metrics

* Profit Margin %
* Return on Assets (ROA %)
* Market Value to Asset Ratio
* Sales to Market Value Ratio
* Investment Score
* Performance Status
* Valuation Scale
* Company Size Level
* Country Region

## Key Insights

* High sales do not always translate to high profitability.
* Oil & Gas generated the highest total sales.
* Diversified Financials recorded the highest average profit margin.
* Technology companies attracted the highest market valuations.
* The Middle East showed strong investment potential.
* Company rank does not always reflect operational efficiency.

---

## Recommendations

* Invest in high-growth technology sectors.
* Explore investment opportunities in emerging high-performing regions.
* Monitor industries with strong profitability trends.
* Consider valuation metrics alongside company rankings when making investment decisions.
